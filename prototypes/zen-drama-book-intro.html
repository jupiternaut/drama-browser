Entrance

书籍入口 / Entrance

皇帝写这本书时，不是在教别人。The emperor was not preaching to others.

他是在夜里校正自己。He was correcting himself at night.

营帐、瘟疫、边境和背叛，都在他身边。Camp, plague, frontier, and betrayal stood around him.

他能控制的，只剩判断。What remained under his rule was judgment.

Six Bones

这套页面的六条骨架

一书。One book.

每次只让一本书占据屏幕。Let one book own the screen.

一镜。One lens.

视角先限定，理解才会变深。A fixed lens makes reading deeper.

一句一译。One line, one mirror.

中文短句之后，英文立刻回应。English answers the Chinese line at once.

一段一图。One scene, one image.

图像不是装饰，是进入文本的门。Images are doors, not decoration.

一观点一故事。One idea, one story.

观点必须有场景支撑。An idea needs a scene beneath it.

一原文一展开。One quote, one unfolding.

短原文之后，要说出伦理后果。A short quote must change conduct.

Core Ideas

四个核心观点 / Four Core Ideas

核心观点一：判断是最后的城墙。

边境失守时，皇帝仍要守住心。When borders shake, the emperor must guard the mind.

短原文：You have power over your mind.

伦理含义是，不把愤怒交给外界代理。Ethically, anger cannot be outsourced to events.

核心观点二：权力先约束自己。

他拥有军队，却反复提醒自己要温和。He owned armies, yet kept rehearsing gentleness.

短原文：Waste no more time arguing.

行动的责任，不是解释善，而是成为善。The task is not to explain good, but to become it.

核心观点三：死亡让欲望变清楚。

瘟疫靠近罗马时，虚荣忽然变轻。When plague neared Rome, vanity became light.

短原文：You could leave life right now.

伦理含义是，用有限生命筛掉虚假任务。Ethically, mortality filters false assignments.

核心观点四：公共责任不是表演。

好人不是姿态，而是一种日复一日的用处。Goodness is not a pose, but a daily usefulness.

短原文：What is not good for the hive...

它改变人的判断：我是否只为自我故事负责。It asks whether I answer only to my private story.

Question For Today

今日问题 / Question for Today

今天有什么事，其实只是在夺走你的判断权。What today is trying to take command of your judgment?

你能不能先不反击，只把判断收回来。Can you first reclaim judgment before striking back?
